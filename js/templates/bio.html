Bio

No one knows more about California's Agriculture than Alfonso A. Guilin. As a youth he worked in his family's melon harvesting and packing operation in Blythe, CA. Later he graduated with a degree in Ag Business from Cal Poly Pomona and rose to prominence as Vice President of the Limoneira Company. After retirement, he became a Catholic Deacon serving mostly immigrant families at Our Lady of Guadalupe Church in Santa Paula, CA. Alfonso's books reflect a lifetime of involvemnt in agriculture conveying hope, struggle, and achievement for his diverse and complex characters.
