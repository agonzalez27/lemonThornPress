Bio

No one knows more about California's Agriculture than Alfonso A. Guilin. As a youth he worked in his family's melon harvesting and packing operation in Blythe, CA. Later on he graduated with a degree in Agricultural Business from Cal Poly Pomona and rose to prominence as Vice President of the Limoneira Company. After retirement, he became a deacon in the Catholic Church serving mostly immigrant farm working families in Our Lady of Guadalupe Church in Santa Paula, CA. Alfonso's books reflect his experiences. The struggle of a person working in the fields with dreams of achieving something more. This is Alfonso's stories and the stories reflected in his books.
